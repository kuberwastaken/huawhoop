1.3 Fitness and health data management

1.3.1 Profile management

To manage your personal profile and improve the accuracy of fitness data, we need to collect your personal profile, including your profile picture, nickname, gender, date of birth (age), height and weight, personal preferences (Android only), and personal emergency information. You can add or modify the information by going to Me > Profile, and save the information. The saved data will be collected. If you do not provide such information, the fitness data we provide to you may be inaccurate, but this does not affect you using personal profile management on your device. You can go to Me > Privacy management > Sync personal info to cloud to stop uploading data to the Huawei Health cloud.

To enable the syncing of your personal emergency information, you can also go to Me > Profile and add, modify, or delete your personal emergency information, including your name, address information, blood type, allergies, healthcare records, and emergency contacts' names and contact numbers. Your personal emergency information will be synced to some smart wearable devices and saved locally after successful pairing. Such data will not be uploaded to the cloud.

To ensure a consistent experience with your health and fitness devices when you switch phones, your app settings (such as the switch status of features like message notifications and screenshot experience enhancement) will be uploaded to the Huawei cloud through encrypted transmission and will be stored in encrypted form, ensuring that these settings can be carried over to your new phone.

1.3.2 Fitness data management

To record, back up, and manage your fitness data, during your workouts, we need to collect your location information, workout route, workout type, workout duration, running form data, step count, calories, distance and ascent, VO2Max, workout heart rate, speed, slope, jump roping data, workout status, workout achievements, wheelchair push count, wheelchair push frequency, average wheelchair push distance, golf activity data, and other workout data, as well as app configuration data (such as your workout goal, heart rate zones, metric and imperial system settings, and metronome settings). Workout types include walking, running, cycling, climbing, swimming, and golf. The purpose of collecting such information is to record and calculate statistics on your daily fitness data and workout route, providing you with rich fitness services.

We will obtain your step count data from the gyroscope sensor in real time. The data will be displayed on the lock screen, Assistant·TODAY, Gallery, and Health step count cards. You can also share your step count and other related data by linking your WeChat, QQ, and Alipay accounts. For details about how your data will be processed, please refer to the privacy policy of the corresponding app.

If you are using an Android device, at the end of a workout, we need to collect your barometer and magnetometer data, optimize your workout route records based on the data, and display detailed altitude data records and climbing data to improve your fitness data experience.

If you are using an iOS device, at the end of a workout, we need to collect your altitude data, and display detailed altitude data records to improve your fitness data experience.

After your outdoor workout ends, we will download road network data for the relevant area to your phone to improve the accuracy of the workout route displayed in the services. Such download will only occur if there is an update to the road network data or if it has not been downloaded previously. Please note that downloading the data over a non-Wi-Fi network will consume a small amount of data (1-2 MB), and the downloaded data will take up minimal storage space. (Such download is not yet supported by the iOS version.)

Providers of the route tracking feature for a single outdoor workout session include AutoNavi Software Co., Ltd., Huawei Software Technologies Co., Ltd., and Google. Your location information needs to be collected and provided to the map service provider during the process. Some service providers also require you to provide your network information. The collection and processing of specific data are subject to the privacy policies of the third party. For details, please refer to the privacy policies of the corresponding map service providers.

To help you analyze your fitness data over time, we will generate different summaries and trends based on the fitness data you have synced to cloud and visualize those summaries and trends in the app.

If you do not provide such information, you can still record your fitness data locally, but cannot store it on the cloud, and may lose the data if you uninstall the app or change your phone. You can go to Me > Privacy management > Sync fitness data to cloud to stop uploading data to the Huawei Health cloud.

When you use the AI fitness feature, we will obtain your gravity sensor data to determine whether the screen is tilted. Such sensor data will not be uploaded to the cloud. (Android only.)

When you use the jump roping feature, we will obtain your accelerometer and gravity sensor data to determine whether the screen is shaking or tilted. Such sensor data will not be uploaded to the cloud. (Android only.)

1.3.3 Health data management

To manage your health data and display relevant data, when you are linking and using your fitness and health device, we need to collect your sleep data, heart rate, height and weight, blood oxygen, blood pressure, blood sugar (except for iOS), body temperature, body fat, menstrual cycle data, emotion data, stress data, physical condition data, lung function data, heart health data, ECG, breathing data, altitude sickness data, heart rate variability data, and the related measurement data and configuration data (such as health goals). The collection of body fat data relies on the collection capability of the device, which may include the weight, body fat, body fat percentage, impedance, BMI, muscle mass, basal metabolism, water weight, water percentage, visceral fat level, bone mineral content, protein ratio, body age, skeletal muscle mass, skeletal muscle mass index, fat balance, muscle balance, body shape, waist-hip ratio, body type, body score, and other calculated and measurement data. Sleep data includes apnea hypopnea index, hypopnea index, air leakage, number of mask-off events, usage duration, and usage score. Heart rate data includes workout heart rate, heart rate, heart rate variability data, high heart rate alert, and low heart rate alert. Waist-hip ratio can be an estimated value or a manually corrected value.  AI body shape analysis covers chest circumference, waist circumference, hip circumference, bicep circumference, thigh circumference, and calf circumference.  Health data is sensitive personal information. The purpose of collecting such information is to measure, record, display, and calculate your health-related data, in order to provide you with rich health services.

If you are using a HarmonyOS-running phone, we will provide you with Health service cards (such as sleep card, diet card, step count card, and fitness card) for data display and instant service access. The Health service cards will preferentially obtain your fitness and health data (such as sleep data, step count, and calories) from the Huawei Health app on the device. If your fitness and health data is stored on the cloud, the Health service cards will obtain your fitness and health data (such as sleep data) from the cloud.

To help you analyze your health data over time, we will generate different summaries and trends based on the health data you have synced to cloud and visualize those summaries and trends in the app.

After the emotional wellbeing risk notification feature is enabled, the short-term and long-term trends in your emotional state data will be used to evaluate your emotional wellbeing status. Emotions are subjective experiences often accompanied by physiological arousal and external expressions and are a psychological phenomenon closely related to the subject's tendencies such as wants and desires, reflecting whether these wants have been satisfied. Emotional changes can trigger a series of physiological responses through the hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal (HPA) axis interacting with the autonomic nervous system. For instance, changes in heart rhythm can occur. By analyzing heart rhythm changes, it is possible to detect emotions. Based on your emotional state and related characteristics data over a period of time, we will notify you about whether there are potential emotional wellbeing risks, and may suggest that you complete a self-assessment questionnaire so that you can further evaluate your emotional wellbeing status. Answers and scores of such questionnaires will not be stored. You can go to Emotional wellbeing, touch the icon in the upper right corner, and go to Settings > Emotional wellbeing notifications to stop evaluating your emotional wellbeing status.

If you do not provide such information, you can still use the health data management feature of Huawei Health, but cannot store your health data on the cloud, and may lose your health data if you uninstall the app or change your phone. You can go to Me > Privacy management > Sync health data to cloud to stop the collection of your health data.

8 Managing Your Personal Information

Huawei highly respects your concerns regarding personal information, and you are provided with the following data subject rights and options.

8.1 Access your information

You can log in to Huawei Privacy Center (https://privacy.consumer.huawei.com/tool?lang=en-us&themeName=blue) using your HUAWEI ID to obtain a copy of your data. To ensure your data security, we need to verify your identity when you request a copy of your data.

8.2 Delete your information

To ensure data security, your data will be linked to your account. Your fitness and health data and personal information will be transmitted to and stored on the Huawei Health cloud server in encrypted form. You can go to Me > Privacy management > Clear all personal data from cloud to delete the data and personal information on the Huawei Health cloud at any time. You cannot retrieve the data after it is deleted. If you delete your account, your data will also be deleted. After you delete the data from the cloud, your fitness and health data will not be synced to the cloud and will remain on your phone or tablet.

You can erase the records of activities you have participated in by logging out of the specific activity or challenge from the app's Activities screen.

You can clear all your fitness and health data by clearing all Huawei health data in phone/tablet settings.

You can also clear all Huawei Health, Health Service Kit, and other service data linked with your account by deleting your account.

You can open the Huawei Health app and go to Me > Settings > Clear cache to clear the cached resources concerned.

8.3 Rectify your information

You have the right to update or correct your personal information processed by us when you need to or find any errors with it.

You can go to Me > Profile to change or update your profile information such as gender, date of birth, height and weight, personal preferences (Android only), and personal emergency information.

8.4 Withdraw your consent

If you want to revoke your authorization for experience improvement, you can go to Me > Privacy management > Stats data and turn off the corresponding switch to withdraw your consent.

If you want to revoke your authorization for analysis and improvement, you can go to Me > Privacy management and disable Analysis and improvement to withdraw your consent.

If you want to revoke your authorization for personalized recommendations, you can go to Me > Privacy management > Personalized recommendations and turn off the Health & fitness recommendations switch to withdraw your consent.

If you want to revoke your authorization for recommendations based on your health data, you can go to Me > Privacy management > Personalized recommendations and turn off the Health data switch to withdraw your consent.

If you want to revoke your authorization for sharing data with third parties, you can go to Me > Privacy management > Data sharing and authorization to withdraw your consent. If you have granted the authorization by logging in to your HUAWEI ID, you can also revoke the authorization in HUAWEI ID Privacy Center.

If you want to revoke your authorization for sharing data with another member in your Health community, you can go to Health community > Member settings or go to Health community > Members and remove the member to withdraw your consent to sharing data with the member.

If you want to revoke data authorization for Smart Training Plan, you can go to Exercise > Plan > Smart Training Plan, touch the four dots in the upper right corner, and touch Revoke data authorization to withdraw your consent.

If you want to revoke data authorization for Event assistant, you can go to Exercise > Outdoor run > My events, touch the four dots in the upper right corner, and touch Revoke data authorization to withdraw your consent.

8.5 Disable service

You can disable the services as instructed below in Huawei Health:

•If you are using an Android device, go to Me > About, then touch Huawei Health User Agreement or Statement About Huawei Health and Privacy in the lower part of the screen and touch Deactivate service.

•If you are using an Android device, go to Me > Privacy management > Huawei Health Notice, then go to DISAGREE > Deactivate service.

•Go to Me > Settings, then touch Deactivate service.

After you touch Deactivate service, you can perform operations based on the following information: If you select Disable service, you will exit Huawei Health, all your personal information cached on this device will be cleared, and the collection of your data from this device will be stopped. If you select Deactivate service, you will exit Huawei Health, the collection of your data from this device will be stopped, and all your personal information cached on the cloud and this device will be cleared and cannot be restored. Exercise caution when performing this operation.

8.6 Get an explanation of the rules

You can go to Me > Privacy management > Content recommendations to obtain an explanation of the basic principles, purposes, and working mechanisms of our automated decision-making/personalized recommendation algorithms.

If you have any further requests, questions, comments, or suggestions regarding your rights as a data subject, please contact us using the contact information provided under the "Contacting Us" section of this Statement.

9 Information Storage Location and Retention Period

9.1 Storage location

The aforementioned information will be sent to and stored on servers in the People's Republic of China.

9.2 Retention period

We will retain your personal information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Statement, and delete or anonymize your personal information beyond the time set out below, except as otherwise required by applicable laws and regulations.

Your fitness and health data obtained in this app will be retained until you delete your HUAWEI ID, disable the services, or actively delete the data.

Your information collected from surveys and device hardware information (device model and device name) collected during updates will be retained for six (6) months from the date of collection.

Personal data used for generating your annual summaries and trends will be retained for two (2) years from the date of collection (in accordance with the requirements set out in laws such as the Personal Information Protection Law).

Information used for the Feedback feature will be retained for up to two (2) years.

Information used for the Help feature will be retained for up to two (2) years.

Data in connection with your participation in an activity or lucky draw will be retained for two (2) years from the end of the activity.

Your transaction records in connection with the membership services will be retained for ten (10) years from the date of collection (in accordance with the requirements set out in the Detailed Rules for the Implementation of the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection and other laws and regulations).

Information collected based on concern reporting will be retained for two (2) years from the date of collection or deleted after your HUAWEI ID is deleted.

Information related to your personalized recommendations will be retained for two (2) years from the date of collection. Information used for product O&M and risk control purposes will be retained for up to five (5) years from the date of collection. Your transaction records will be retained for ten (10) years from the date of generation. Under this item, the deletion of all the information is independent of whether your account is deleted.

In the event that we discontinue our products or services, we will notify you in the form of a push notification, announcement, or by other means, and delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period of time.
