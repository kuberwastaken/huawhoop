1 What Data Do We Collect About You?

As part of Huawei Health, we shall collect and use your personal data only for the following purposes stated in this Statement. Personal data refers to any information that enables us to identify you as a user.

We will collect and process the following data:

Account information

•HUAWEI ID account information: such as HUAWEI ID account identifier, profile picture, username, gender, and date of birth.

Personal profile information

•Personal profile information: such as certain account details (gender and date of birth), height, weight, and app settings.

Your gender and date of birth are used for providing you with better exercise suggestions. Your height and weight are used to calculate and increase the accuracy of various health and fitness metrics. If you do not give such information, or decide to give us false information, our algorithms may calculate and provide inaccurate suggestions and metrics for you.

If you create a group or group event using the Groups feature, we will process the group information (such as group identifier, name, label, group picture, home country, user profile pictures, password, and creation and modification timestamps) and group event information (such as event identifier, name, type, goal type, goal value, and ranking type), along with your account identifier.

Huawei Health's friend invitation capabilities in Health Community are provided by the Friends and contacts feature of HUAWEI ID. When you send invitations, we need to match the invitee's phone number, email address, or account information with information about existing HUAWEI IDs, to allow for appropriate delivery of the invitation messages. We will not store any of the data used for this account matching process.

Other Health Community users will be able to see your nickname and profile picture and send invitations to add you as a friend using your email address, phone number, or account identifier if you have enabled the Friends and contacts feature in HUAWEI ID. When you send invitations to others, they will be able to identify you by your nickname, profile picture, and the email address or phone number that you used for registering your HUAWEI ID.

Fitness data

•Fitness data: such as your device location, workout track, workout type (such as walking, running, cycling, climbing, basketball, and swimming), workout duration, step count, wheelchair push count and frequency, distance covered, calories burnt, altitude, maximum oxygen uptake, exercise heart rate, exercise form, workout plan data, race information, diving data, diving messages, and other workout and activity analysis data. Some fitness data may reveal aspects of your health, such as exercise heart rate and data about certain activity types, for example, wheelchair-based activities.

Fitness data from your wearable device is synced to Huawei Health when the device is connected to Huawei Health. The synced data will vary between different wearable models. You can also add some data yourself, such as workout records.

Health data

•Health data: Your health data, as well as the analysis results and reports of such data, will be processed, including sleep data, sleep breathing data, heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV) derived metrics such as heartbeat interval, stress score, breathing rate, emotional wellbeing values (pleasant, neutral, or unpleasant), altitude sickness assessment, menstrual cycle data, body surface temperature, body shape, body composition (including body fat, body resistance, BMI, blood oxygen saturation (SpO2), muscle mass, metabolism, water rate of body, visceral fat, protein, and bone mineral), blood pressure data, respiration, weight and weight management data (such as data connected to a light fasting plan), diet and weight loss information (including name of the food products you have eaten, nutritional facts and analysis, workout/activity you have completed to consume the calories, and target and the consumed calories) processed for weight management purposes, and completed tasks (such as status for completing tasks and type of challenge, as well as daily smiles, water consumption, and breathing exercises depending on which tasks you complete). If you record your sleep sound data with your mobile device, the sleep analysis data will be synced to the cloud based on your authorisation; however it will not include the sleep sound recordings, which will only be processed on your device.

If you use the Cycle Calendar card, we will process your menstrual cycle data. When you conduct a Stress test for the first time, a wellness questionnaire is used for calibrating your test results. The calibration is done using a calibration value derived from your answers. The calibration value is shared with your Huawei wearable and then erased from the Health app. Your answers to the questionnaire are never stored.

If you use the Arterial Stiffness Detection service, your vascular data (such as pulse wave velocity, pulse wave form, pulse wave conduction velocity, PPG, measurement time as well as vascular elasticity result and grade) will be processed. If you use the ECG service, your ECG data (such as ECG measurement time, result, wave form, average heart rate, and symptoms) will be processed to provide the service. If you use the Sleep Breathing Awareness service, your sleep time, heartbeat interval, and blood oxygen saturation level will be processed to provide the service. If you use the Pulse Wave Arrhythmia service, we will process your pulse wave measurement time, waveform, and analysis results, such as suspected arrhythmias.

If your wearable supports the Emotional wellbeing feature, your emotional wellbeing values (pleasant, neutral, or unpleasant) will be displayed in the app. You can disable the collection of this data at any time by going to Emotional wellbeing settings and disabling Additional emotions/stress records.

Health data from your wearable device is synced to Huawei Health when the device is connected to Huawei Health. The synced data depends on the services you are using and the capabilities of your wearable. You can also add some data yourself, such as sleep, mood, and diet records.

Device and network information

•Device and network information: such as your MAC address, device serial number, IMEI or other device identifier (depending on your mobile system), device type, and personal settings will be collected for device management, including for establishing Bluetooth connections, managing device configurations, firmware updates, and displaying the source device of the data. Your IP address, carrier information, system settings, wireless network identifier (SSID), network type, and network connection will also be collected in order to improve your network connection experience. We will process the list of map apps on your device to provide different app options for navigation.

Service usage information

•Service usage information: such as device and app usage data, including the data about how you open or close the app, and view pages, touch buttons, and enable or disable features in apps, the device running status, comments, emergency information and contacts (not stored on our servers), as well as order and purchase information, membership information, consent records, approved user agreement information, and coupons information.

If you share a device with other users, such as a weight scale, we will collect the device identifier, account identifiers, and nicknames of users who use the device.

If you use the Feedback feature in app settings, we will process your device information, contact information, your feedback, as well as the attachments and app logs you upload (optional). If you decide to attach the app log file, please note that it includes data about your latest workouts, including data about exercise type, heart rate, and calories burnt.

When you use HUAWEI Wallet on your wearable device, you can first scan the QR code to add a membership card through the HUAWEI Wallet entry in Huawei Health. Once the membership card is successfully added, the membership card data will be synced to your wearable device. Huawei Health does not process any card information or personal data related to the membership card.

If you have enabled AppGallery from the Devices settings, you can access the AppGallery Privacy Statement as well as disable AppGallery service at any point by going to Devices > AppGallery > Settings.

If you use Health Community and consent to sharing your personal profile and health and fitness data to your friend, we will process your HUAWEI ID account information (including nickname), your friend's nickname, data sharing permission settings, and status of the invitation.

If you use Health in a visitor mode without logging in to your HUAWEI ID account, we will collect and process the following data:

•Device information: such as device identifier (depending on your mobile system), operating system (depending on your device) version and build number, ROM version, serial number, screen size and resolution, country/region code, language setting, and Huawei Health app identifier and app version.

•Fitness data: basic fitness information such as step count, calories burnt, workout duration, and active hours. These data points may be generated even when a Huawei wearable device is not used.

•Network information: such as mobile country code (MCC) and mobile network code (MNC).

•Service usage information: such as approved user agreement information and timestamps of usage activities.

1.1 Which Permissions Are Necessary?

Android devices: To provide you with the service, Huawei Health needs to access the Internet and device information, such as device identifier, and requires the following permissions: Physical activity (to track steps and activity), Phone (to obtain the phone status when receiving calls, rejecting incoming calls, and receiving incoming call information after pairing with the wearable device, and for eSIM card activation), Storage (Media and files) (to upload local audio to the wearable device, upload routes, download maps, store workout and other photos, and read photos from the local storage when e.g. setting the watch face of the wearable device, sharing photos, or changing avatars), Location (to map outdoor workouts, provide location-based weather reports, discover and add new Bluetooth devices on certain operating systems, and share location with other users (e.g. your group and Health Community members)), Microphone (to analyse sleep sounds and input food names by voice into your diet log), Camera (to take workout photos or sweeping shots, scan QR codes, and identify and record nutritional values from photos of nutrition facts labels), Contacts (to sync contacts and frequently used contacts to the wearable device, add emergency contacts, and add contacts to groups), Messaging (to sync SMS messages to the wearable device), Calendar (to sync workout plans to your calendar), Nearby devices (to discover and add new devices), and Call logs (to sync calls to the wearable device). The information accessed from your device based on the Microphone, Contacts, Calendar, Phone, Messaging, and Call logs permissions is neither processed nor stored on our servers.

iOS devices: To provide you with the service, Huawei Health needs to access the Internet and device information, such as device identifier, and requires the following permissions: Motion & Fitness (to track steps and activity), Location (to map outdoor workouts, provide location-based weather reports, and share location with other users (e.g. your group and Health Community members)), Microphone (to analyse sleep sounds and input food names by voice into your diet log), Camera (to take workout photos or sweeping shots, scan QR codes, and identify and record nutritional values from photos of nutrition facts labels), Contacts (to sync contacts and frequently used contacts to the wearable device, add emergency contacts, and add contacts to groups), Calendars (to sync workout plans to your calendar), Local Network (to transfer, over Wi-Fi, large files such as firmware updates, watch faces, offline maps, golf maps, music, and app stores), Bluetooth (to discover and add new Bluetooth devices, use Bluetooth configuration features, and obtain exercise data from Bluetooth devices), and Photos (to store workout and other photos and read photos from the local storage when e.g. setting the watch face of the wearable device, sharing photos, or changing avatars). The information accessed from your device based on the Microphone, Contacts, and Calendars permissions is neither processed nor stored on our servers.

You will receive the necessary permission requests based on the specific requirements of your device's operating system. You can manage the permissions on your device by going to the permission management screen for the app from system Settings. If you do not grant the required permissions, we may be unable to provide the associated feature, or it may not function optimally.

Following the completion of your outdoor workout and based on your prior authorisation, road network data relevant to your route may be downloaded and stored on your mobile device to enhance workout route accuracy. Other workout indicators, such as distance, remain unaffected by this feature.

If you wish to set up custom watch faces, such as album watch faces, on your wearable device, we will process your account identifier and device information to enable you to use this feature. Additionally, the photos you select will be accessed from your device and transmitted to the watch face of your wearable device for display. These photos will be stored locally only and will not be processed or stored on our servers. This feature requires the Storage (Media and files) and/or Camera permissions.

Huawei Health may send you different push notifications (e.g. about steps count, daily targets, sleep reports, health tips, etc.). You can manage such notifications from Me > Settings > Notification management. In addition, we need to access the list of apps installed on your device to enable you to manage which of these apps may have their messages displayed on your wearable device. This app installation information is neither processed nor stored on our servers.

1.2 Is Your Data Collected Through Third-Party Technologies?

There are no third-party technologies, such as SDKs, integrated with the app that enable remote access and processing of your data by any third parties, whether acting on their own or on our behalf.

2 How Do We Use Your Data?

To perform our contract with you

To provide you with Huawei Health functions and fulfil contractual obligations, this app must collect and process your data for customer relationship management, maintenance, software and system updates, user identification, and diagnostic and repair purposes. If you have the capacity to enter into an enforceable contract, the aforementioned processing of your personal data is based on your contract with us. You can find the User Agreement by going to Me > About > Huawei Health User Agreement in the app. This purpose requires processing of your HUAWEI ID account, device and network, and service usage information. Your personal profile and fitness and health data shall be uploaded to Huawei Health cloud only after you give your explicit consent in the app. For information on processing of your personal data for customer support purposes, please read the Statement About Customer Support and Privacy.

Based on your consent

Syncing fitness and health data to the cloud

Your personal profile and fitness and health data, which includes sensitive information about you, shall be uploaded to Huawei Health cloud only after you give your explicit consent in the app. You can go to Me > Privacy management in the app at any time to erase the data from the cloud (see Section 5.4) and/or cease further collection and processing (see Section 5.5). When you sync data to the cloud, we will not process your fitness and health data for any other purposes than storing it in the cloud and making it available for viewing and analysis from your mobile device, and for providing additional Huawei Health features you have explicitly consented to. To help you analyse your data over time, we will calculate e.g. different trends from the data you have synced to cloud and visualise those trends in the app. Additionally, we will calculate insights through statistical correlation analysis of data such as sleep patterns, heart rate, stress level, step count, and activity records. For example, this analysis may suggest that improved sleep quality could result from a reduction in stress level. You can find such trends and insights from, for example, the Health insights section of the app.

Health Service Kit

Health Service Kit is a fitness and health data platform service that allows you to manage and share your data across different services. When Health Service Kit is enabled in the Huawei Health app, your personal profile information, and fitness and health data in the Huawei Health app is accessed, uploaded to the Health Service Kit cloud, and made available for authorised apps to access and modify with your consent. For more information about how Health Service Kit processes your data, please read the Statement About Health Service Kit and Privacy.

Apple Health

On iOS devices, you can authorise the Huawei Health app to read data from and write data to Apple Health. You can also choose which specific data categories to include in your authorisation (for example, Active Energy, Blood Oxygen, Body Fat Percentage, and Body Mass Index). You can change your authorisation settings by opening Apple Health, touching your profile picture, and going to Privacy > Apps and Services > Huawei Health.

Fitness and health data sharing

Huawei Health supports sharing of fitness and health data with other Huawei health and fitness apps (such as Arterial stiffness detection, Sleep apnoea test, Pulse wave arrhythmia analysis, and ECG) and third-party health and fitness apps. Such data sharing is conducted only based on your prior authorisation. If you enable the cloud syncing, the data from that app will be automatically synced to the Huawei Health cloud. You can adjust your data sharing settings by going to Me > Privacy management > Data sharing and authorisation.

Huawei Health app activities and challenges

If you take part in different activities and challenges in Huawei Health, we need to process your health and fitness data (such as daily steps, calories, and sleep time, depending on the challenge) to calculate your results and achievements. In addition, your score and HUAWEI ID profile information, including your profile picture, will be visible to all participants. For us to calculate your results and achievements, syncing your fitness and health data to cloud may be a prerequisite for participating in such activities and challenges.

Personalised fitness plans

If you create a personalised fitness plan, we will process your profile data (such as gender, age, height and weight), fitness data as well as your fitness plan and analysis data (such as workout records from the past month and best workout performance from the past six months, target result, plan progress and periodic training plan report) with your consent for the purpose of creating you a personalised fitness plan. You may withdraw your consent any time by ending the plan and removing your fitness data from the cloud as described in Section 5.5.

Event Assistant

Event Assistant is a one-stop race management centre. You can add official marathon events or create custom races, sync them to your watch, and set distances and target times.

Event Assistant processes the following information:

•Race information: such as race search input, selected event name, location, time, distance, target performance, and route.

•Location information: Your approximate location is used to recommend nearby events when you have granted location access for the Huawei Health app (requested separately). If you have not granted location access, you can still search for races manually. Location information used by Event Assistant is not stored on our servers.

You can disable Event Assistant at any time, as described in Section 5.5.

Smart Training Plan

Smart Training Plan is a personalised, dynamic training system built on sports science and individual data. It intelligently creates, analyses, and adjusts training programmes by integrating diverse data and conditions.

Smart Training Plan processes the following information:

•Health information: including heart rate, heart rate variability, sleep data, breathing data, stress levels, blood oxygen, body fat percentage, and menstrual cycle data.

•Fitness information: including speed, workout heart rate, workout duration, workout route, workout distance and ascent, workout performance, cadence, workout type, calories, workout status (such as running ability index, training load, and recovery time), running form data, VO2max, personal preferences, other workout data, and fitness courses and plans.

•Basic profile information: including date of birth, sex, weight, and height. Weight and height information is mandatory as it'll be used to determine your body mass index (BMI) so as to provide safe, suitable workout courses for you along with effective fitness tips. In addition, your age and sex are used to ensure better training results throughout the programme. If this information is inaccurate, the algorithm might not provide workout courses or fitness tips accurate to you and that may increase your risk of injury.

•Location information: Your location information is used to assess weather, temperature, humidity, and UV index data when you have granted location access for the Huawei Health app (granted separately). You can still use this feature even if you don't grant location access, but results might be less accurate. Location information won't be stored on our servers. Elevation information will also be used to assess fitness adaptation.

•Event information: including event name, location, time, distance, and goal performance.

You may disable Smart Training Plan at any time, as described in Section 5.5.

Health Clovers

If you enable a plan in the Health Clovers service in Huawei Health, we will process your completed tasks and type of task as well as personal profile and health and fitness data (such as step count, workout data, sleeping records, daily smiles, daily water consumption, and breathing exercises, depending on the tasks you complete) with your consent for the purpose of providing the service and giving you advice on healthy living habits. You may withdraw your consent any time by disabling Health Clovers service as described in Section 5.5.

Health Community

If you use Health Community, you may share your personal profile and health and fitness data to your friend or family member. Your personal data may be shared only if you send or receive a friend invitation and consent to sharing your selected data items to your friend or family member.

My groups

If you use the Groups feature and participate in a group, you'll automatically be included in the group's activity rankings based on your steps, workout duration, and total distance covered. Also, if you participate in a group event, you'll be included in the event-specific activity rankings. These may be based on steps, total distance, pace, workout duration, cycling power, or moderate- and high-intensity activity duration, depending on the event type. Your ranking data will be visible to other members, along with your nickname and profile picture. You can update your nickname and profile picture in accordance with Section 5.2. To be included in group and group activity rankings, you must enable cloud sync for fitness data by going to Me > Privacy management and enabling Sync fitness data to cloud. You can leave any group you have joined in the Groups feature by going to Me > My groups, finding the group and opening its homepage, selecting Settings from the upper right corner, and selecting Leave group. If you leave a group or event or if it is deleted by the creator, the ranking data will be erased.

If you consent to Location sharing during a group event, other event participants will be able to see the precise real-time location of your mobile device along with your nickname and profile picture. You can also participate in any event and appear in the rankings without sharing your location. You can stop sharing anytime by touching the Close button for location sharing in the group event view. If you stop sharing in the middle of an event, other members will be able to see your last known location until the event ends or you leave the event. Once you leave the event or it ends, your location data will no longer be visible to anyone. Please note that starting or stopping location sharing from outside your home or another identifiable place may allow others to infer personal details, such as your residence or workout routine. If you want to reduce identifiability, consider using a generic nickname and a non-personal profile picture. You can update your nickname and profile picture in accordance with Section 5.2.

Marketing campaigns

If you participate in marketing campaigns in Huawei Health, such as lotteries or lucky draws, we will process your participation records and the winner's contact information in accordance with the specific terms and conditions applicable to such campaigns.

Analysis and improvement

If you have enabled the Analysis and improvement feature and contributed with your data to help us improve Health app and your user experience, we will access and use data for:

•App improvement: Account details (e.g., age and gender), network data (like IP address), device type, and app usage patterns (such as screens viewed, buttons touched, features enabled or disabled, and operation frequency) are used to measure traffic and usage trends to help us understand how our users use and interact with the service and support ongoing improvements. This data will be also used to provide Personalised recommendations, if you have separately consented to it.

•Creating workout routes: Location data from your outdoor workouts, along with average speed, pace, and elapsed time (relative timestamp from workout start) is used to identify routes that are frequently followed. These popular routes may be turned into workouts that are readily available to all users. Each route will be manually reviewed before published in the Huawei Health app. No specific user can be identified from the created routes and the routes are created by aggregating your de-identified data with other users' data. For your data to be included, you must also have enabled cloud sync for fitness data by going to Me > Privacy management and enabling Sync fitness data to cloud.

You can withdraw your consent to the processing for Analysis and improvement as described in Section 5.5 below.

Personalised recommendations

When the Personalised recommendations feature is enabled, your Huawei Health app experience is tailored to provide content—such as articles, activities, deals, and offers—based on your predicted preferences. To achieve this, we create and use segments, which are groups of users with similar estimated interests and characteristics, to determine the content shown to you. For example, if you frequently engage in running workouts, we may predict your interest in running-related content, such as articles.

•By analysing user preferences, we aim to offer products and services that better align with individual needs and expectations, enhancing the user experience while supporting our business objectives through more relevant offerings.

•To personalise content, we utilise account information (such as account identifier, age, and sex), device details (such as mobile and wearable device models), and app usage data (such as screens viewed, buttons interacted with, features enabled or disabled, and usage frequency). Health data and specific workout data, including details of individual exercise sessions or performance metrics, are not used in this process.

•The Personalised recommendations feature is only available if you have consented to the use of your data for Analysis and improvement purposes (see above). If you prefer not to have your data used for this purpose, you can withdraw your consent to Personalised recommendations as explained in Section 5.5 below. If this feature is disabled, the same amount of content will be provided; however, it may be less relevant to you, as it will be based on general interests rather than personalised insights.

Consent records

Please note that your consent preferences are linked to your HUAWEI ID. As a result, any version of the Huawei Health app accessed using the same HUAWEI ID may automatically apply your previously selected consent settings. For instance, if you uninstall and reinstall the app, your consent status will remain consistent with the choices you made earlier.

Based on legitimate interests

Information security and fraud prevention. We use your personal data for information security purposes and to detect or prevent various types of misuse of services and frauds based on our legitimate interest in order to provide you with secure and reliable services. This purpose requires processing of your HUAWEI ID account, personal profile, device and network, and service usage information.

Service provision for younger users. If you have a limited ability to enter into an enforceable contract (for example, if you are below the age of majority and the law in your country does not permit you to enter into enforceable contracts), we may not be able to process your personal data on the basis of performing a contract with you. In such cases, we rely on legitimate interests to provide you with the Huawei Health service, including customer relationship management, maintenance, software and system updates, user identification, and diagnostic and repair purposes. Such processing is necessary for us to pursue our business interests in providing, supporting, and maintaining Huawei Health for users who cannot enter into an enforceable contract. This also serves the legitimate interests of our users in being able to use Huawei Health and embark on their personal fitness and health journey. This purpose requires processing of your HUAWEI ID account, device and network, and service usage information. Your personal profile and fitness and health data shall be uploaded to Huawei Health cloud only after you give your explicit consent in the app.

To comply with legal obligations

We will process your comments in Health Community based on legal requirement, in order to efficiently identify and remove harmful content and language from the comments before they are made available to your friends in Health Community.

2.1 How Do We Safeguard Children?

Children are allowed to use Huawei Health by logging in with their HUAWEI ID child account ("child account") created by their parent or guardian. When a user logs in with a child account, and as a result is identified as a child user, Huawei Health provides different safeguards to ensure age-appropriate data processing and content:

•Parents or guardians need to explicitly consent to the processing of personal profile and health and fitness data of a child account user in Huawei Health when the service is accessed for the first time.

•In order to protect children from any harm caused by advertising, we do not use children as a specific target group for any marketing activities.

•Child account users cannot sync and share their fitness and health data in Huawei Health with third-party services.

NOTE: If a child is using our services without being logged in with a child account, we are not capable of providing the child related safeguards provided by the service. We encourage all parents and guardians to create a child account for their child before they are allowed to start using any HMS apps or services. If a child is allowed to use Huawei Health without a child account, it is to be done under sole supervision of the parent or guardian.

3 How Long Do We Store Your Data?

We will retain your personal data for no longer than is necessary for the purposes defined in this Statement.

We will process and retain your data for the duration of the customer relationship. Your personal profile and health and fitness data, which you have synced to Huawei Health cloud, will be stored as long as you do not erase it.

We shall retain data as follows:

•Personal data processed for contract management purposes will be retained for up to three (3) years after your HUAWEI ID is deleted.

•Order information will be retained for seven (7) years from the date the transaction was made.

•Personal data that is used for analytics and improvement purposes as well as for personalised recommendations will be retained for up to twelve (12) months from the date of collection, unless you have withdrawn your consent to such processing. The network data used for improving your network connection experience will be retained for no longer than seven (7) days.

•Personal data processed for feedback to be submitted by you through the Huawei Health app will be retained for six (6) months.

•Ranking data in the Groups feature will be retained until the group event is closed or you leave the group, unless the group or group event is deleted by the creator, in which case the data will be erased immediately.

•The de-identified location data from your outdoor workouts along with average speed, pace, and elapsed time (relative timestamp from workout start) will be kept for six (6) months after collection to identify and generate popular workout routes.

•Information about invitations sent via the HUAWEI ID Friends and contacts feature will be retained until you delete your HUAWEI ID account.

•Data backups and app logs processed to ensure the security of your data and services will be retained for up to six (6) months from the date of creation.

Once the retention period expires, we will delete or anonymise your personal data in due time, unless otherwise required by applicable laws and regulations.

4 How Do We Share Your Data?

Data sharing with third-party apps

Huawei Health supports sharing of fitness and health data with third-party health and fitness apps. Such data sharing is conducted only based on your prior authorisation. You may also authorise the Huawei Health app to receive data from these apps. If you enable the cloud data syncing for any given app, the data from that app will be automatically synced to the Huawei Health cloud. You can adjust your data sharing settings by going to Me > Privacy management > Data sharing and authorisation.

We recommend you to read carefully the privacy statements/policies of the third-party apps/services you decide to share your Huawei Health data with and, for example, to check whether such apps/services may process your data outside of the EU/EEA. We only share and transfer the data based on your prior authorisation. The data is transferred from our servers to our partners' servers. Please note that the local laws of such countries/regions may not provide the same level of protection of your data as in the EU/EEA.

Huawei Health enables you to make your Huawei sports watch available for other health and fitness apps. Such third-party apps can integrate and access your watch (e.g. to get heart rate or other sensor data, to send notifications to your watch, etc. depending on the scope of the authorisation) only after your explicit authorisation. You can adjust such device settings for each app separately by going to Settings > Device capabilities.

Weather reports

If your wearable device supports the Weather reports feature, your city-level location is shared with our weather service providers located in the EU operating under contact and acting on behalf of us. You may disable the Weather reports feature by going to Devices in this app, selecting the device you have paired with, and going to Weather reports.

Shared devices

If you share a device with other users, such as a weight scale, your personal information may become visible to other users of the device under certain circumstances (such as when the device does not know to which user to allocate the measurement result).

Maps

Depending on your operating system either Petal Maps or Google Maps provides the service for generating and mapping your outdoor workout routes based on your device's location. Google Maps use is governed by Google's Privacy policy available at http://www.google.com/policies/privacy.

IT services and business operations

We may also share your data in the following circumstances:

•Our third-party vendors, who provide us with IT (including cloud-based) services, need to process your information. These vendors supply the core IT infrastructure that supports our service. All such third parties are operating under contract and acting on behalf of us and are located in the EU/EEA. The servers where we store your data are located in Germany.

•Our affiliate companies may need to process your data, such as HUAWEI ID information, to support specific and limited daily business operations (e.g. ship and deliver a prize to you, and help with other local activities). All such third parties are operating under contract and acting on behalf of us and are located in the EU/EEA.

•We may share your data in individual instances with external auditors, legal advisers, or other professional advisers, where necessary in the course of the services that they render to us.

•We may share your data in response to a legal process or request from a competent authority according to applicable laws or in connection with a legal proceeding or process.

5 What Are Your Rights and Options?

You have the following rights and options:

5.1 Access your data

You can request information and a copy of your personal data that we have collected and stored in relation to Huawei Health by going to Settings > HUAWEI ID > Privacy centre > Manage Your Data > Request Your Data on your Huawei device, or logging in to https://privacy.consumer.huawei.com/tool using your HUAWEI ID in a browser on your computer or mobile device and then clicking or touching Request Your Data.

For additional access requests, please contact us.

5.2 Rectify your data

To keep your data up to date and accurate, you can:

•Access and modify your Huawei Health data by opening the Huawei Health app and going to Me > Profile.

•Access and modify your HUAWEI ID data, including your account nickname and profile picture, by opening the Huawei Health app, going to Me, and touching your profile picture.

•Access and modify your nickname in a group by going to Me > My groups, finding the group and opening its homepage, selecting Settings from the upper right corner, and touching Nickname in group.

5.3 Port your data

You can port your personal data that you have provided to us in relation to Huawei Health in a commonly-used machine-readable format by going to Settings > HUAWEI ID > Privacy centre > Manage Your Data > Request Your Data on your Huawei device, or logging in to https://privacy.consumer.huawei.com/tool using your HUAWEI ID in a browser on your computer or mobile device and then clicking or touching Request Your Data.

5.4 Erase your data

You may at any time:

•Erase all fitness and health data from the cloud by going to Me > Privacy management > Clear all personal data from cloud in the app. If you erase the data, the data will be permanently deleted from the Huawei Health cloud, but it remains on your mobile phone.

•Go to Me > Settings and select Deactivate service. You will be given two options:

- DISABLE SERVICE: This stops further data collection and deletes all data stored locally on your device. However, your data will remain stored on our servers.

- DEACTIVATE SERVICE: This stops further data collection, deletes all data stored locally on your device, and also permanently deletes all your data from the Huawei Health cloud.

•Erase your data collected in relation to activities and challenges you have participated in by logging out of the specific activity or challenge from the app's Activities screen.

•Erase all personal data from your mobile device's app settings by clearing Huawei Health data.

•Erase device information and device sharing information regarding a paired device, such as a weight scale, from the cloud by unpairing the device.

•Delete your HUAWEI ID by opening the Huawei Health app, going to Me, touching your profile picture, and going to Account security > Security centre > Delete account. As a result, your personal data will be erased from Huawei Health, Health Service Kit, and other services, which are linked with your HUAWEI ID account.

•Delete your data that has been processed to provide a plan in the Health Clovers service by disabling the Health Clovers service. To do so, open the Health Clovers screen, touch the dots in the upper right corner, and go to About > Disable service.

•Object to the processing of your data, as described in Section 5.6. As a result, we will erase the data in the scope of a valid objection if we no longer have legal basis to continue the processing.

•Contact us if you think the processing of your personal data is unlawful and your data should be erased.

We will erase or anonymise your personal data without undue delay when necessary based on your aforementioned actions and in accordance with the retention periods as described in Section 3.

5.5 Withdraw your consent

Your fitness and health data shall be uploaded to Huawei Health cloud only after you give your explicit consent in the app. You can withdraw your consent by going to Me > Privacy management and adjusting the Data syncing management settings in the app:

•Sync personal info to cloud: Controls the syncing of your personal profile information.

•Sync fitness data to cloud: Controls the syncing of your fitness data.

•Sync health data to cloud: Controls the syncing of your health data.

Your Huawei Health data is uploaded to the Health Service Kit cloud only after you give your explicit consent in the app. You can withdraw your consent by going to Me > Privacy management > Health Service Kit.

You can withdraw your consent to the processing of your data for analysis and improvement purposes by going to Me > Privacy management and disabling Analysis and improvement.

You can withdraw your consent to the processing of your data for Personalised recommendations by going to Me > Privacy management > Content recommendations and disabling Personalised recommendations.

You can disable Event Assistant from the Event Assistant page by touching the dots in the upper right corner and choosing Revoke authorisation.

You can withdraw your consent to the processing of your data for Smart Training Plan from the Smart Training Plan page by touching the dots in the upper right corner and choosing Revoke data authorisation.

You can leave any group you have joined in the Groups feature by going to Me > My groups, finding the group and opening its homepage, selecting Settings from the upper right corner, and selecting Leave group.

You can withdraw your consent for location sharing in any group event by touching the Close button for location sharing in the group event view. If you stop sharing in the middle of an event, other members will be able to see your last known location until the event ends or you leave the event. Once you leave the event or it ends, your location data will no longer be visible to anyone.

You can withdraw the authorisation to share your data with other services by going to Me > Privacy management > Data sharing and authorisation in the app.

You can withdraw your consent to processing your personal data for the creation of personalised fitness plan by ending the plan by touching the dots in the upper right corner and touching End plan, and deleting your data from Huawei Health cloud by going to Me > Privacy management in the app.

You can withdraw your consent for joining a Health Clover plan, by opening the Health Clovers screen, touching the dots in the upper right corner, and going to About > Disable service.

You can withdraw your consent to processing your personal data for the Huawei Health service by going to Me > Settings > Deactivate service > Disable service.

If you have shared your personal profile and health and fitness data in Health Community to your friend or family member, you may withdraw your consent to the data sharing by removing your friend or family member from your Health Community. To do so, go to your friend's or family member's health profile, touch the profile picture of the member to access their profile, and touch Remove.

5.6 Object to processing

If you wish to object to the processing of your data, which is based on our legitimate interests, please contact us.

When making the request, please specify the scope of the request and provide us with the email address or phone number that you use to log in to your HUAWEI ID.

We will contact you to verify your identity in order to proceed with your request.

5.7 Restrict processing

You have the right to restrict the processing of your data in the following circumstances:

•Your data is unlawfully processed, but you do not want to erase it.

•You have a legal claim that you need to establish, exercise, or defend, and you have requested us to keep your data when we would not keep it otherwise.

•You have contested the accuracy of your personal data and the accuracy of your data is pending our verification.

•Your request for objection is pending our verification.

You may at any time restrict the processing of your personal profile and health and fitness data to your device only by going to Me > Privacy management and disabling the data syncing in the app.

For other requests related to the restriction of data processing, please contact us. When making the request, please specify the scope and the grounds for the request and provide us with your email address or mobile number that you use to log in to your HUAWEI ID.

We will contact you to verify your identity in order to proceed with your request.
